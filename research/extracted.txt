Key fact 5: Dropping Robert Sánchez (whose direct kicking was crucial in Chelsea's Club World Cup win over PSG) created a no-win situation for both goalkeepers — "By making the change, the danger is Rosenior has undermined both of his goalkeepers. Sánchez's confidence will be hit by confirmation he is no longer Chelsea's No 1 while Jörgensen must recover from unravelling in the biggest game of his career."

Key fact 6: Chelsea had a 3rd goal ruled out for tight offside, and "A 3-2 defeat would not have been the worst result... It did not even feel insurmountable when Kvaratshkelia whipped in the fourth goal in the 86th minute."
